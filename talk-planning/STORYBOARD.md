# Storyboard

This file defines the talk scene by scene.

Each scene should be short. The text on screen should support the spoken argument, not replace it.

## Scene Template

```md
## Scene 01

Eyebrow:

Title:

On-screen text:

Spoken point:

Background:

Transition:

Notes:
```

## Draft Flow

## Scene 01

Eyebrow: Opening

Title: Human agency under algorithmic mediation

On-screen text: What happens when AI systems become part of ordinary judgment, trust, and dependence?

Spoken point: Introduce the central research agenda.

Background: Black

Transition: Fade in

## Scene 02

Eyebrow: Research Problem

Title: Some harms are hard to name

On-screen text: Relational. Cumulative. Epistemic. Emotional.

Spoken point: Explain why existing legal and technical categories often miss these harms.

Background: Emotional dependency or AI companion visual

Transition: Slow crossfade

## Scene 03

Eyebrow: Method

Title: Translate between law, computer science, and public accountability

On-screen text: From concepts to measurable systems.

Spoken point: Explain the interdisciplinary method.

Background: Paper front pages or research archive

Transition: Paper/card fade

## Scene 04
